About ParentsGuideToAiBot

ParentsGuideToAiBot is a simple automated script that posts new articles from this blog to the r/parentsguidetoai subreddit and the Parents Guide to AI Facebook group.

What it does

When a new article is published on this blog, the bot posts a link to it on Reddit and Facebook so community members on those platforms see it in their feeds without having to check the blog directly.

  • Reddit: posts a link submission to r/parentsguidetoai with the article title and URL
  • Facebook: posts to the group with the article summary and a link, which Facebook expands into a preview card using the page's metadata

When it runs

The bot runs once per day. It checks for articles that have been published since the last time it ran and posts any that haven't been shared yet. If no new articles have been published, it does nothing.

What it will and won't post

  • It only posts articles that are new since the last run — it will never re-post something already shared
  • It posts the article title and URL as-is, without modification
  • It does not post draft articles or articles with a future publish date
  • It does not comment, vote, moderate, or interact with replies in any way

Who operates it

This bot is operated by Yvan Boily, the author of A Parent's Guide to AI. It is a personal script running on a personal machine — it is not a commercial service or a third-party automation tool.

Something wrong?

If the bot posts something incorrectly, posts a duplicate, or behaves unexpectedly, please open an issue on GitHub or leave a note in the relevant Reddit or Facebook thread.
