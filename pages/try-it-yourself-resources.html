AI Tools to Try

Free tools, no account required to start:

  • Microsoft Copilot — Works in your browser without signing in. The easiest starting point, and the most relevant for VSB families.
  • Google Gemini — Google's AI. Strong at research and working with documents. Free with a Google account.
  • ChatGPT — OpenAI's conversational AI. The most widely used in the world. Free tier is genuinely useful. Minimum age: 13.
  • Claude — Anthropic's AI. Strong at reasoning and extended writing tasks. Free tier available. Minimum age: 13.
  • Perplexity — AI-powered search that cites its sources. Useful for staying current on topics and verifying claims.

BC and Canadian Resources

Schools and education policy

  • BC Ministry of Education — AI in Education — Province-wide policy and guidance for K-12. Check at the start of each school year.
  • VSB Digital Literacy and Use of AI in Education — The Vancouver School Board's current approach to AI.
  • VSB Copilot 13+ in Secondary Schools — VSB's managed AI program for eligible secondary students.
  • ERASE (Expect Respect and A Safe Education) — BC's school-based safe schools reporting system.

Media literacy and privacy

  • MediaSmarts — Canada's centre for digital and media literacy. Well-sourced parent resources on AI, algorithms, and online safety. Updated regularly.
  • Office of the Privacy Commissioner of Canada — Canada's federal privacy regulator. Guidance on your rights regarding AI tools and data protection.

If something goes wrong

These resources are worth bookmarking before you need them.

  • Take Back Your Images — Free service that creates a digital fingerprint of an intimate image so it can be detected and removed from participating platforms.
  • NeedHelpNow.ca — For young people in Canada dealing with intimate images circulating online. Step-by-step guidance on reporting and removal.
  • Cybertip.ca — Canadian Centre for Child Protection reporting line for online child sexual exploitation material, including AI-generated content.
  • Kids Help Phone — 1-800-668-6868 (call 24/7) or text CONNECT to 686868. Counsellors available around the clock.
